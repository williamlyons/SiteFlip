Grading Policy

We recognize that students enter CSE247 with a wide variety of backgrounds. Therefore, CSE247 strives for a non-competetive learning environment. Our aim is to help you learn, not filter you out. To avoid putting you in competition with your classmates, we will not compare students in the determination of grades for CSE247. No means are used and there are no curves

Your semester score is computed as the following weighted average:

Component Weight
Participation 7%
Extension Writing 10%
Labs 20%
Homework 3%
Exam 1 20%
Exam 2 20%
Final Exam 20%

Participation

Participation credit will be given for using WUTexter in class and filling out the course evaluation at the end of the semester.

Homework

Homework will be assigned thoughout the semester. You will receive credit simply for completing the assignment on-time, provided you attempted to do each of the problems, even if you get the answers wrong.

Exams

There are two in-term exams planned and one cumulative final exam.

Labs

Labs are due by the date indicated on the course syllabus. Your lab must be your own solution. Labs are due as posted on the course calendar and syllabus. Your lab score is the fraction of labs you have completed as compared to those assigned. Thus, your lab score will be 100% if all labs are completed as described above.
